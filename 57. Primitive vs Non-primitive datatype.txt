#Primitive DataType:
    - The datatype that has a specificized size.
        - eg: byte(1 byte), short(2 bytes), int(4 bytes), long(8 bytes), float(4 bytes), double(8 bytes), boolean() and char(2 bytes)
    - Primitive DataType are predefined.



-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
#Non-Primitive DataType:
    - Non-Primitive DataType are predefined.
    - They are created by the programmer.
    - All Non-Primitive datatype have same size, depending on the size of the object.
