--> Authentication:
    - Authentication is the process of verifying the identity of a user, system, or application.



---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
--> Authorization:
    - Authorization is the process of determining if a user has the necessary permissions to perform a specific action or access a particular resource.
    - Authorization is done by setting roles (like admin has more permissions than normal users)



---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Note: In spring boot we use "spring security" for "Authorization" and "Authentication" purposes.
